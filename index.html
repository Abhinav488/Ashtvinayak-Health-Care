Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions under one roof

General Physician

Complete primary healthcare services for all ages. From common illnesses to preventive care, we diagnose and treat a wide range of medical conditions with personalized attention.

Diabetes Management

Specialized diabetology care including blood sugar monitoring, diet planning, medication management, and education for living well with diabetes.

Gynecology Care

Comprehensive women's health services covering prenatal care, reproductive health, menstrual disorders, and menopause management with sensitivity and expertise.

Chest & Asthma / Allergy

Expert treatment for respiratory conditions including asthma, COPD, allergies, and breathing difficulties with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic approaches.
